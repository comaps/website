CoMaps contributors and volunteers [Anton](https://codeberg.org/patepelo) and [Bastian](https://codeberg.org/gedankenstuecke) joined monthly meeting of the *Tech & Innovation Working Group* of [Humanitarian OpenStreetMap](https://www.hotosm.org/en/) on April 14 and presented the work of CoMaps. In their brief talk *CoMaps: a community-made map app for Android, iOS & Linux*, they gave some background about how CoMaps came together as a community, the applications themselves, and how some of the newly developed features of CoMaps could be used in humanitarian settings.

You can [find their slide deck online](https://talks.tzovar.as/2026-04-14-hot/). The source code to edit and create them those slides [is on Codeberg](https://codeberg.org/gedankenstuecke/talks/src/branch/pages/2026-04-14-hot). If you want to build on those slides, they are released under a Creative Commons BY-SA 4.0 International license.
