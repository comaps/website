CoMaps contributors and volunteers Diego Miguel Lozano and Álex attended the 2026 edition of [*esLibre*](https://eslib.re/2026/es/), which took place on April 17/18 in Melide in Spain.
Together they presented CoMaps in their presentation *CoMaps Aplicación de mapas offline centrada en la privacidad*, talking about the history of the project, how it is governed, its features and the code base of the project.

You can [find their slide deck on Codeberg](https://codeberg.org/dmlls/CoMaps-esLibre26/src/branch/main/CoMaps-esLibre26.pdf), along with the source code to edit and create them! If you want to build on those slides, they are released under a Creative Commons BY-NC-SA 4.0 International license.
