Together with UN Maps Community Ambassador [Silvina Meritano](https://maps.un.org/news/introducing-un-maps-community-ambassadors), CoMaps contributor [Bastian](https://tzovar.as) attended the 22nd edition of the *Festival Latinoamericano Instalación Software Libre* in Córdoba, Argentina to give a talk titled *Mapear para y desde la comunidad: usar y contribuir a OpenStreetMap*. In it, they covered what OpenStreetMap is, why we need it, and different ways of how to make use of the data in OSM and how to contribute to it.

As part of this, they showcased CoMaps as a simple way of accessing OSM data offline, including for navigation, and demonstrated how CoMaps can be used to easily make contributions back to OSM, to help improve maps for everyone.

You can [find their collected resources, along with the slide deck online](https://talks.tzovar.as/2026-04-25-flisol/).
