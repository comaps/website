CoMaps has been listed on the page for *[European & Open Source Alternatives](https://european-alternatives.cloud/)* for a while, but since a few days our project has now also a trust score.

And we could not be happier about it: With a trust score of 9.6 out of 10 points, **CoMaps has become the top-ranked alternative to Google Maps and Apple Maps**!
We are very proud that this puts us side-by-side with *OpenStreetMap* itself.
And there is already work going on to improve some of the points that the review raised regarding reliability, to further improve our score.

You can [read more about our scoring and how it compares to other apps in the map category](https://european-alternatives.cloud/en/browse?category=maps).
